Our Menu

A friendly neighborhood bar and grille where everyone knows your name.

Founded by Bob Bevington, a lifetime restaurateur who got his start at the 19th Hole in Chippewa as a teenager. By 16 he was the kitchen manager, by 18 he was made the bar/general manager. Bob remained at the 19th Hole through high school and college before helping locate, renovate and develop a second location, Pier 19 in Bridgewater, managing both locations for three years. Bob then bought the Valley Hotel on route 51, completely renovated the building and opened his dream restaurant, Grand Valley Inn, in 1997. In 2000, he launched Brady's Run Grille & Guesthouse, located directly across the street from the inn. Bob felt it was important to keep a portion of the original name, Brady's Run Hotel, so it was a simple transition.

  • We have become well known for our signature “Crunch” sandwiches. The Crunch is potato chip encrusted chicken, deep fried and served sandwich style in original, buffalo or tuscan seasoning.
